The Best Movies According to Chelsea

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dead Poets' Society

This was Robin Williams at his best. It's a good reminder to stop taking life so seriously.

Matilda

I never get tired of watching this movie. It's inspiring, suspenseful, funny, and so quotable.

Mean Girls

Tina Fey wrote this classic with no anticipation for how huge it would become. For me, Janis was the hero of this movie.
